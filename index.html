My Hobbies

10-05-2021
Susan Si

As a college student, I have a lot of free time to do things that I like. During my own time, I love listening to music. My favorite kind of music is Hip-hop, and I listen to rap songs in different languages, such as Korean, Chinese, and English. I found that various kinds of languages make the rhymes of rap songs unique.

My other hobby is visiting museums. I think history is fun, and all historical stories which made the world becomes what it is now are really meaningful. Therefore, when I am traveling, I always visit museums to learn about the cultures. Museums are always quiet and organized, so I feel calm while I am walking around the exhibits. I enjoy how museums use pictures, sounds, and sometimes even touch interactions to educate people.

Last but not least, I love playing video games. During the pandemic, I stayed at home and tried several video games. League of Legends became my favorite game. Before I joined this game, I thought that the game was very hard, and it was just people who use magic skills to win combats. However, I played League of Legends for months now, and I am good at the game. I also think the game is interesting when you can play with your friends as a team.

My Journey to MSU

10-10-2021
Susan Si

Before I came to Michigan, I was in a high school located in Virginia. It was an all-girls school, and it only has around 100 students. I was an international student, so I studied hard to improve my English. My high school was a life turning point for me because I found out my true self. During high school, I learned how to be courageous and positive. I joined several clubs, such as the Honor Council, the Recycling Team, and the Art Club.I made friends all around the world.

I chose to come to MSU because I liked this school after I learned about the culture here. Our school has strong sports teams, and East Lansing is a safe and diverse city. Students and teachers seemed to be very welcoming and kind. I wanted to choose Media as my major, and I knew that MSU could provide all the resources that I will need to achieve my goals in the future.

Now I am a junior, and I loved my experience in college. I have worked hard in all my classes, but I enjoyed my life as much as I can. I felt no regret in my three years at MSU
